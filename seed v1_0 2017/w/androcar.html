All about ANDROCAR
SEED presents AndroCar

The event AndroCar comprises of total 4 days workshop where students will be taught about various technologies such as Bluetooth communication , Arduino App development , brief knowledge of various sensors and modules such as Ultrasonic Sensor , L298N motor driver , etc.
(A) 4 day workshop of AndroCar comprises

Day 1: 1. Component Distribution
2. Description of various components , their use and application.
3. Detailed knowledge about each component and its working/interfacing.

Day 2: 1. Assembling and integrating various components
2. Chassis completion
3. Arduino Uno : IDE platform introduction and basic level coding on Arduino Uno.

Day 3: 1. Arduino Advance Coding / Coding of AndroCar
2. Test run manually using IDE platform..
3. Ultrasonic mode coding.

Day 4: 1. App development
2. Final test run and completion certificate to team.

All the four workshops will be conducted in the month of Sep - Oct ( apart from duration 22nd Sep 17 to 2nd Oct 17 )
(B) AndroCar kit will be given to the team which will comprise of :-

(i) Arduino Uno Board - 1
(ii) Bluetooth module - 1
(iii) Motor driver IC - 1
(iv) DC motors/wheels - 4
(v) 12V Li-ion battery - 1
(vi) 9V Alkaline battery - 1
(vii) Ultrasonic Sensor - 1
(viii) A to B cable - 1
(ix) Jumper wires and connectors

The total cost of workshop along with the kit is Rs. 2000 /- per team Maximum number of students/teams - : 4 members
On the successful completion of the workshop and test run , each member of the team will be given a participation certificate of the event and will be eligible to take part in the main completion/event of AndroCar at Zealicon .
