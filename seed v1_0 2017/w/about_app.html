About the App - S.E.E.D
Society of Electrical and Electronics Engineering Department (S.E.E.D), JSS brings you this application to give you every bit of college and university information.
ELECTRINO gives you regular updates regarding events and workshops held in JSS.

INNOVATION informs you about updates in the electronic world.

UNIVERSITY provides you previous papers and university exam syllabus verified by A.K.T.U.

EXTRAPEDIA is something different, it tells you what all extra you could do to launch your career in an innovative direction.

BOOKS from all point of views of competitive genre and university books are provided and can be saved offline using the google drive options.

EXAMS updates taking place all around for government jobs, CAT, GATE etc are provided for your betterment.

TIME TABLE , ACADEMIC CALENDAR are too added for JSS students.

UPDATES section for students to keep them updates regarding all the new information in the college relating to sessional dates, any new event or activity.
Issue books from Knowledge Sharing Club (K.S.C.), JSS and so much more.
Download and make your life easier with on the go notifications and image updates.
